Buy Me a Coffee

Currently in Italy there are more inmates than what the system is designed to host. Still, the situation is probably worse as the official regular capacity of places does not account of missing spots.

After consulting the numbers from the Ministry of Justice, I noticed that - despite OpenData initiatives - the ministry's data is provided in a format that is not particularly convenient. Associations that deal with the rights of detainees - such as Antigone - are therefore forced into tedious data collection work, sometimes manual, which leaves a lot of room for errors and consumes many resources and time. To address this issue, I initiated a project to collect and systematize the data made available by the Ministry in order to make them more useful to those who need them. All the numbers included on the current page are automatically updated every 15th of the month. The methodology and data collected so far are freely accessible on GitHub.

Prison overcrowding, where do we stand compared to the regulatory capacity

After a sharp reduction due to anti-COVID measures, the numbers of people deprived of their liberty in Italy have returned to pre-pandemic levels.

Source: Elaboration on data from the Italian Ministry of Justice

These situation hints towards a situation of absolute emergency for Italian prisons. On average, across all detention cetners there are inmates for every regular spot. However, this situation is probably an optimistic estimate.

Regular Capacity is not Real Capacity

The Italian Ministry of Justice publishes monthly bulletins containing information regarding the presence of inmatess inside the about 189 detention centers across the country. However, data regarding the actual capacity of detention centers is not provided with the same times.

The most recent data containing information about the actual capacity from detention centers is from . In that moment in time there were avilable spaces in the Italian detention system. That means that places that were supposed to be available, were not. That is approximately one every spots.
